The Tale of Two Voids

Mestre's Challenge

Abandoned Railways

Miles of disconnected iron arteries cutting through the city. Once the lifeblood of logistics, these railway zones now act as physical barriers, dividing neighborhoods and gathering rust. They represent immense linear potential for parks, cycling highways, or community markets.

Marghera's Challenge

Industrial Voids

Monumental structures left hollow by deindustrialization. These massive concrete cathedrals stand empty, waiting to be repurposed. They offer the necessary square footage for large-scale innovation hubs, artist collectives, or indoor vertical farming.

The UrbanHub Solution

A transparent, democratic pipeline to turn urban decay into community assets.

1

Citizen Seeds Idea

Anyone can propose a transformation for an abandoned spot. A "Seed" describes the vision, the logic, and the estimated budget.

2

Community Upvotes

The community reviews, discusses, and upvotes Seeds. Only the most supported ideas gain momentum to reach the review threshold.

3

Funding & Execution

Once the threshold is met, the Seed is formally reviewed by the Municipality and opened for Investor funding.
